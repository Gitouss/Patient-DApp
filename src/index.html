Welcome to Patient-DApp

Welcome to Your Patient Decentralized Application, basically, this application demonstrates the use of secured Blockchain in Healthcare. Patients's records are been stored privately to the blockchain for security and data integrity. I am working on integrating all the medical records into this DApp for the optimization of supply-chain in healthcare. Patient-DApp runs on top of Ethereum platform, using solidity as the programming language. Secure Remote Password Protocol was used for authentication to mitigate against any form of brute-force attack on the application.
